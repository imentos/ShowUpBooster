## Full Description

### The Problem
30% of people don't show up to appointments, property showings, and events. The friction of downloading an app or manually adding calendar reminders means people forget or lose commitment.

### The Solution
ShowUpBooster eliminates friction with iOS App Clips:

✅ **Zero install required** - Recipients tap a link and confirm in 10 seconds via App Clip  
✅ **Smart reminders** - Auto-scheduled notifications 24h, 2h, and 30min before the event  
✅ **One-tap RSVP** - Simple "Confirm & Remind Me" button  
✅ **Works everywhere** - Share via Messages, email, QR codes, or any messaging app  
✅ **Full app available** - Hosts can download the app to create and manage event links  

### Perfect For
- 🏠 Real estate agents (open houses, showings)
- 📅 Small businesses (appointments, consultations)
- 🎉 Event organizers (meetups, parties)
- 🍽️ Restaurants (reservations)
- 🏥 Medical offices (patient appointments)

### How It Works

**For Hosts (Full App):**
1. Download ShowUpBooster from App Store
2. Create event with details (title, location, date/time)
3. Tap "Generate Link" and share via Messages/email
4. Track confirmations

**For Recipients (App Clip - No Install!):**
1. Tap the shared link or scan QR code
2. App Clip card appears instantly
3. See event details on map
4. Tap "Confirm & Remind Me"
5. Get automatic reminders before the event

### Tech Stack
- SwiftUI for modern, native iOS experience
- App Clips for frictionless access
- UserNotifications for smart reminders
- MapKit for location visualization
- iOS 16+ for full app, iOS 14+ for App Clips

### Why We Built This
As an indie developer tired of event no-shows, I wanted a solution that didn't force people to download an app. App Clips are perfect for this - instant access, zero friction, maximum commitment.

## Launch Strategy

### Pre-Launch (1-2 days before)

**Build your hunter network:**
1. Join Product Hunt community discussions
2. Comment on other launches
3. Build rapport with active hunters
4. Consider asking top hunter to "hunt" your product (boosts visibility)

**Prepare assets:**
- [ ] Write maker comment in draft
- [ ] Create 5 screenshots
- [ ] Record demo video (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Test App Clip link works perfectly
- [ ] Prepare 3-5 canned responses for common questions

**Alert your network:**
```
Message to friends/network:

"Hey! I'm launching ShowUpBooster on Product Hunt [Tuesday/Wednesday]. 

It's an event confirmation app using App Clips - pretty cool tech.

Would mean a lot if you could upvote + comment when it goes live. 

I'll send the link when it's up! 🚀"
```

### Launch Day

**Best time:** Tuesday or Wednesday, 12:01 AM PST  
(Product Hunt days run 12:01 AM - 11:59 PM PST)

**Hour 1 (12:01 AM - 1:00 AM PST):**
- [ ] Submit product immediately at 12:01 AM
- [ ] Post maker comment within 2 minutes
- [ ] Share with 5-10 close friends/co-founders immediately
- [ ] Ask them to upvote + leave genuine comment

**Hour 2-4 (1 AM - 5 AM PST):**
- [ ] Share in your Twitter/X network
- [ ] Post in relevant Slack/Discord communities
